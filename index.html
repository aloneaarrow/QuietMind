Privacy Policy for QuietMind

Last Updated: February 1, 2026

Introduction

QuietMind ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our QuietMind mobile application ("App").

By using QuietMind, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

1. Information You Provide

Account Information:

  • Email address (for account creation)
  • Display name (optional)

Tinnitus Profile:

  • Tinnitus type (ringing, buzzing, etc.)
  • Affected ear (left, right, both)
  • Severity level (1-10 scale)
  • Estimated frequency

Usage Data:

  • Daily severity logs you submit
  • Listening session duration
  • Sound preferences

2. Automatically Collected Information

Device Information:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • App version

Analytics Data (Firebase Analytics):

  • App usage patterns
  • Feature engagement
  • Crash reports (Crashlytics)

3. Payment Information

If you subscribe to QuietMind Premium, payment is processed by Google Play Store. We do NOT collect or store:

  • Credit card numbers
  • Bank account information
  • Billing addresses

We only receive confirmation of your subscription status from RevenueCat/Google Play.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  1. Provide Services: Deliver sound therapy and tracking features
  2. Personalize Experience: Remember your preferences and settings
  3. Improve the App: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
  4. Customer Support: Respond to your inquiries and issues
  5. Send Notifications: Daily reminders (if enabled by you)

Data Storage and Security

Where Your Data is Stored

  • Local Storage: Most data is stored locally on your device
  • Cloud Storage: Account and sync data is stored on Firebase (Google Cloud)
  • Location: Servers are located in the United States and European Union

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication (Firebase Auth)
  • Regular security audits

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information.

We may share data with:

  1. Service Providers:
    • Firebase (Google) - Authentication and database
    • RevenueCat - Subscription management
    • Google Analytics - Usage analytics
  2. Legal Requirements:
    • When required by law or legal process
    • To protect our rights or safety

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Portability

You can request a copy of your data at any time.

Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data by:

  1. Going to Settings > Account > Delete Account
  2. Contacting us at privacy@quietmind.app

Opt-Out Options

  • Notifications: Disable in Settings > Notifications
  • Analytics: Contact us to opt-out of analytics

Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained while account is active
  • Deleted accounts: Data removed within 30 days
  • Anonymized analytics: Retained indefinitely

Children's Privacy

QuietMind is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you are accessing QuietMind from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the App
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending a notification for significant changes
